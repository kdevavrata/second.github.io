About Us

Our dance institute is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Gaudiya Nritya. We offer expert training in this graceful dance form to students of all ages and skill levels. NGCB Foundation is a mission of promoting Bengal's very own classical danceform, Gaudiya Nritya

Performances

Witness the mesmerizing performances by our talented dancers as they bring alive the enchanting stories and emotions through the expressive movements of Gaudiya Nritya.

Achievements

We take pride in the achievements of our institute and the recognition we have received for our dedication to preserving and promoting Gaudiya Nritya. Our dancers have performed on prestigious stages and received accolades from esteemed critics.
